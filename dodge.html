Performance and Features

The Dodge Challenger is available in multiple engine configurations, ranging from a 3.6L V6 to the monster 6.2L HEMI V8 in the Hellcat and Redeye models. The V8 variants can deliver up to 797 horsepower, propelling this beast from 0 to 100 km/h in around 3.5 seconds. It features an 8-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual for purists who love the feel of shifting gears.

Inside, the Challenger combines modern tech with a driver-focused cockpit. Premium materials, advanced infotainment with Uconnect, and a customizable digital cluster give the interior a luxurious yet performance-oriented feel.

Market Price

The market price for a Black Dodge Challenger Coupe can vary based on the model and specifications.
